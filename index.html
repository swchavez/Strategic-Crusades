Strategic Crusades

Welcome to Strategic Crusades, a premier political consulting firm based in Mississippi. Our firm is led by Stephen Chavez and Ernest Clifford Hadley, two seasoned political consultants who bring a wealth of experience and expertise to every campaign they work on.

With a combined 10 years of experience in political strategy and communication, Stephen and Ernest have a deep understanding of the unique challenges and opportunities of running successful political campaigns. They have worked with a wide range of candidates, from local office seekers to statewide candidates, and have a proven track record of helping their clients win elections.

At Strategic Crusades, we believe that every campaign is unique and deserves a tailored approach that addresses its specific needs and goals. We work closely with our clients to understand their vision, build a comprehensive strategy, and execute a winning plan that gets results. Whether you're seeking local, state, or national office, we have the experience and expertise to help you achieve your political aspirations.

If you're considering a run for public office, we invite you to contact us and learn more about how we can help you build a successful campaign. Our team of political professionals is here to provide you with the guidance and support you need to achieve your goals. Let's work together to make a difference in your community!
